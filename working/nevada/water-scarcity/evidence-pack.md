## What changed since the July 14 pack

This is a **full re-collection**. Discovery now combines NELIS keyword search
with the **LCB official Subject Index of Bills** (professional staff indexing
under WATER, WATER RIGHTS AND APPROPRIATION OF PUBLIC WATERS, STATE ENGINEER,
DROUGHT, IRRIGATION, DATA CENTERS, and related headings) plus new keyword
clusters from the Phase 2 constituent proposals (desalination, pipeline,
cooling, partial abatement).

- Old set: 88 bills. New set: **165 collected**, tiered by relevance:
  - **86 core** (water or data-center policy is the point of the bill)
  - **14 adjacent** (a real but partial water/big-user angle)
  - **65 context** (found by broad terms or omnibus indexing; kept for audit,
    excluded from headline counts)
- **Policy set (core + adjacent): 100 bills** — the basis for all headline
  numbers below.

Every attempt to restrain them died: 2025 AB77 (deny abatements — first committee), 2023 SB394 (cap abatements at $500k — passed the Senate 14–7, died in the Assembly), and 2025 AB385 tried the *opposite* (block local cooling-tower bans — first committee). ## Data limits

1. Discovery is keyword + official subject index — strong, but not a proven complete universe.
2. The record shows **where** bills stopped, never **why**.
3. Committee Yea votes are inferred (membership minus recorded Nay/Absent); marked in source data.
4. Party labels come from official NELIS legislator directories (100% of 6,636 ballot rows matched).
5. Context bills are kept in the pack for audit but excluded from headline counts.
