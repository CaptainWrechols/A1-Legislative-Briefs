# Appendix G — Which Finished Measures Were Amended Along the Way

For the 48 measures that finished the process (45 laws and 3 enrolled resolutions), the Legislature's records show both the text *as introduced* and the text *as enrolled* (the final version), along with any amendments adopted in between. 35 of the 48 were amended before finishing — the version that became final was not identical to the version first proposed. This appendix reports whether the official record lists adopted amendments; it does not judge whether a change strengthened or weakened a measure. Failed bills are not listed because they have no final version to compare.
