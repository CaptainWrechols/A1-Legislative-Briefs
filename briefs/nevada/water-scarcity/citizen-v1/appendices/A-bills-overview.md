# Appendix A — All 88 Water Bills at a Glance (2019–2025)

This appendix lists every bill in the evidence set: 88 water-related bills and resolutions from Nevada's 2019, 2021, 2023 and 2025 regular legislative sessions. The set was found through water-keyword searches of the Legislature's records, so it is broad but not guaranteed complete; the **Strong match?** column marks the 70 bills whose titles are clearly about water (the other 18 touch water only partly). "Where it stopped or finished" describes the last stage a bill reached — a bill's *first committee* is the committee it was sent to in its starting chamber.

**Theme labels used below (full names):**

| Short label | Full theme name |
|---|---|
| Permits | Who gets water and how permits are decided |
| Groundwater & wells | Rules for underground water and wells |
| Conservation | Saving water (conservation rules and incentives) |
| Planning | Planning for growth and future water supply |
| Colorado River | Colorado River and deals with other states |
| Funding | Money for water projects and agencies |
| Clean water | Clean drinking water and pollution |
| Disputes & oversight | Who referees water disputes (courts and the State Engineer's office) |
| Other | Other bills that touch water |

**Bills per session in this set:**
