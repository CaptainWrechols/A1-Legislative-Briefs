# Appendix E — How Far Each Bill Traveled

This appendix shows the milestone path of every bill: whether it cleared its first committee, passed its first (origin) chamber, passed the second chamber, and what happened at the end. "Stopped" marks the stage where the bill's progress ended; "—" means the bill never reached that stage. A stage counts as passed only when the record shows a winning vote or equivalent action — a few raw milestone flags in the source data over-marked second-chamber passage, and those were corrected against the action history. The "Latest recorded action" column quotes the Legislature's last entry for the bill.
